Marguerite Delacroix — The Art of Matter

Trained in Fine Arts and haute cuisine, the Chef turns the bistro into a living canvas. Each dish is born as a pictorial composition, balancing textures and natural pigments. "Cooking is sculpting the ephemeral." Her journey took her through Michelin-starred Parisian kitchens and workshops in Florence, until she found in São Paulo the perfect stage for the fusion of art and flavor.

— L'émotion à table
